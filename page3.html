Are you ready for the ultimate virtual retreat? Separation is a journey into solitude, desolation and mystery. Atmospheric and ambient, with unhurried meditative gameplay, Separation is an escape from the noise of an overconnected world. It is a place to explore and reflect, a refuge for the soul, a hidden place of windswept sand and ruins.

Let your spirit wander alone, to find the truth within The Temple Of Sorrows... 

Awakening, a distant voice bekons: "Don't go back to sleep... It's been so long since I had a visitor..."

Each obelisk lit, brings you closer to the truth...

As the new dawn fades, you walk a path of light... 

What lies within the Temple Of Sorrows? The mysterious voice urges you on. 

Wander freely, explore, search the desolate landscape for the forgotten sorrows that will unlock the temple. The crows will be your guide. 

Take your time, enjoy the solitude, the sound of the wind and the ever shifting play of light. Experience the presence of 'being there' in VR.

  • Featuring a soundtrack of over forty minutes of ambient electronica by Vector Lovers.
  • Around two hours complete play through time.
  • Atmospheric, ambient, minimal aesthetic.
  • Exploration / puzzle based gameplay
